Ramonify is a data-driven personal finance dashboard designed to help students
understand their spending in simple language.

Users can upload a transaction-level CSV to:
- See monthly income, expenses, and net cash flow
- Identify top spending categories by month
- Receive plain-English budgeting tips
- Get overspending warnings
- Download summarized data for further analysis
- View simple forecasts of next month's cash flow

This project focuses on accessibility, financial literacy, and practical insights.
especially for first-generation and student users.

**Tech Stack:** Python, pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, Matplotlib, Gradio
